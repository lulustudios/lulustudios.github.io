Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in BuildQuote- Estimate (the "App"). We believe in absolute data sovereignty; your data belongs to you and stays with you.

Summary: BuildQuote does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, construction project details, or quote calculations to any external servers.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed to function as a standalone tool. All data entered into the App, including but not limited to project names, measurements, material costs, and final quote calculations, is stored locally on your device.

  • No Personal Information: We do not require or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.
  • No Registration: There is no account creation process or login requirement.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking software to monitor your behavior within the App.

2. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with third-party services only for the purpose of facilitating one-time lifetime purchases:

In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

If you choose to purchase the full version of the App, the transaction is processed via the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We use RevenueCat to manage these entitlements. During this process, an anonymous identifier is used to verify your purchase status. No project data or personal calculation data is ever shared with RevenueCat or the App Stores.

3. Data Storage

All calculations and quote data reside within the App's private storage on your local machine/device. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be permanently removed unless you have manually exported your quotes.

Legal Disclaimer

Professional Advice: The calculations provided by BuildQuote are for estimation purposes only. Construction costs can vary significantly based on location, market fluctuations, and specific site conditions.

Accuracy: While we strive for accuracy in our formulas (e.g., Total Cost = Σ(Quantity × Unit Price) + Overhead), the App is provided "as is." The developer shall not be held liable for any financial losses, architectural errors, or project delays resulting from the use of these estimates.

Verification: Users are strongly advised to verify all quotes with professional contractors and local material suppliers before entering into binding legal or financial agreements.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
